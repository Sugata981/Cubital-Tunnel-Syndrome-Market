### Growing Burden of Nerve Compression Disorders

Cubital tunnel syndrome, caused by compression of the ulnar nerve near the elbow, has become increasingly common among individuals involved in repetitive arm and elbow movements across industries such as manufacturing, information technology, automotive production, and healthcare. The growing prevalence of sedentary work environments combined with repetitive occupational tasks has accelerated demand for effective diagnostic and therapeutic interventions. Increasing patient awareness regarding symptoms such as numbness, tingling, and hand weakness is also supporting early medical consultation and treatment adoption worldwide.
